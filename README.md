## Project Overview

Homi was created to simplify the moving experience by giving users a way to plan their space digitally. Users can:
- Create a digital version of their new space
- Add and arrange furniture in 3D
- Visualize how their items will fit before physically moving them
- Save and share designs for collaboration

---

## Features
- **3D Room Builder:** Model your actual room dimensions.
- **Furniture Catalog:** Add items from a pre-built or custom furniture library.
- **Drag-and-Drop Controls:** Easily move, rotate, and resize furniture.
- **Save & Load Layouts:** Store your room designs for future editing.
- **Export Views:** Take screenshots or share your room setup with others.

---

## Technology Stack
- **Frontend:** React (with Three.js for 3D rendering)
- **Backend:** Node.js + Express
- **Database:** MongoDB
- **3D Models:** Blender/GLTF assets
- **Hosting:** GitHub Pages / Vercel
